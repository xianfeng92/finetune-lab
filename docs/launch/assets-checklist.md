## Copy Guardrails

- Do say: `loss 降了，不代表模型学会`
- Do say: `held-out probe 和 case diff 才能看行为是否改变`
- Do not say: `finetune-lab 是通用微调框架`
- Do not imply: `SIM run 等于真实微调`
- Do not imply: `LiteRT-LM base-only 结果等于 same-LoRA runtime parity`
- Do not overclaim model quality; always frame current runs as teaching evidence and reproducible artifacts
